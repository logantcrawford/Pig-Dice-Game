Pig Game Rules:

  • The game has 2 players, playing in rounds.
  • In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as they wish. Each result get added to their current score and the last number is logged below the player's score.
  • If the player rolls a 1, all their round score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn.
  • The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his round score gets added to their score. After that, it's the next player's turn.
  • The first player to reach the defined "Score to Win" points wins the game.
